IPT TOKEN WHITEPAPER

1: Introduction

The Infinite Potential Token(IPT) is designed to create passive income through farming and staking partnerships while utilizing a deflationary mechanism to store and build wealth. By implementing scheduled burn of 1% per quarter for two years, IPT addresses inflation associated with staking and farming rewards.

2: problem

In the DeFi space, inflation often arises from staking and farming rewards entering the supply, which can dilute the value of tokens over time. IPT aims to solve this problem through strategic deflationary measures, including schedule burns, token buybacks, and vaulting reserves tokens.

3: The Solution

IPT integrates several mechanisms to ensure a long-term value preservation:

Deflationary Mechanisms:

chedule burns reduces supply to counteract inflation from staking and farming rewards

passive income:

users can earn rewards through staking and farming partnership, allowing for synergistic compounding of multiple tokens.

Wealth Storage:

The deflationary nature of IPT enables holders to store and grow their wealth over time
